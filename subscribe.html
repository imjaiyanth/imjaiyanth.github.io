Our History

Founded in 1998, CORE Construction & Designs has been providing quality construction services for over 20 years. Our team has completed a wide variety of projects including commercial buildings, residential homes, and industrial facilities. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.
